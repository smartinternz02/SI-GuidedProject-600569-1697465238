Frequently Asked Questions

What is FelinAI?

FelinAI is a cutting-edge AI system designed for classifying cat species based on images. It employs advanced deep learning techniques to identify and categorize various feline breeds.

How accurate is FelinAI in classifying cat species?

FelinAI strives for accuracy, achieving a classification precision of over 95%. It has been trained on extensive datasets of diverse cat images to ensure reliable results. While we currently focus on seven specific breeds, we're committed to enhancing our system to include more breeds in the future.

What types of cat species can FelinAI identify?

FelinAI excels at recognizing a select group of cat breeds, including the African Wildcat, Blackfoot Cat, Chinese Mountain Cat, Domestic Cats, European Wildcat, Jungle Cat, and Sand Cat. As we continue to develop our technology, we aim to expand the list of identifiable breeds.

Can FelinAI identify mixed-breed cats?

Yes, FelinAI has the capability to recognize mixed-breed cats among the seven specific breeds it currently supports. However, we're actively working to broaden our recognition to include more diverse cat breeds and mixed breeds.

How do I use FelinAI to classify a cat's species?

Using FelinAI is a simple process. Upload an image of the cat you wish to classify, and our AI system will analyze the image to determine if it belongs to one of the seven recognized breeds. You can access this feature on the Predict page of our website.

Is FelinAI accessible to everyone?

Absolutely! FelinAI is designed to be accessible to anyone interested in identifying cat species. Whether you're a cat enthusiast, researcher, or simply curious about your feline friend's breed, FelinAI offers a user-friendly experience for everyone.
